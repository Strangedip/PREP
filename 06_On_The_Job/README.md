# On the Job — Developer Lifecycle After You Get Hired

> **Level**: ALL — SDE1 through Principal
> **Purpose**: Bridge the gap between interview prep and thriving in a real engineering role
> **Complements**: [ROADMAP](../ROADMAP.md) (interview ladder), [00_Interview_Prep](../00_Interview_Prep/README.md)

---

## Why this section exists

The rest of this repository prepares you to **get hired**. This section prepares you to **succeed after Day 1** — onboarding, debugging production, on-call, writing RFCs, code review, performance reviews, and choosing the IC vs Engineering Manager path.
