# One-on-Ones & Performance Reviews

> **You are here**: On the Job — Tech Lead / EM
> **Roadmap**: [Developer Master Roadmap](../ROADMAP.md) | **Prerequisites**: [EM Track](06_Engineering_Manager_Track.md), [Tech Lead Conflict](../00_Interview_Prep/Levels/Tech_Lead_Conflict_and_Performance.md) | **Next**: [Stakeholder Updates](09_Stakeholder_Updates_and_Visibility.md)

1:1s and performance cycles are where careers accelerate or stall. This playbook is for **Tech Leads and EMs** running them — and for ICs who want to drive the conversation from the other chair.

---

## Weekly 1:1 (30–45 min) — not a status meeting

Status belongs in standup/tickets. 1:1 is for **relationship, growth, and early risk**.

### Agenda template

| Minutes | Topic |
|---------|-------|
| 0–5 | Human check-in (energy, blockers outside work if offered) |
| 5–15 | Their agenda (always first) |
| 15–25 | Feedback both ways (specific, recent) |
| 25–35 | Career / skills / promotion evidence |
| 35–45 | Commitments (yours and theirs) — write them down |

### Questions that surface truth

- What is draining you that I am not seeing?
- Where do you want to be in 18 months — IC depth, lead, EM?
- What feedback have you been waiting to give me?
- Which project should we stop or cut scope on?
- Are you getting enough focus time? What should we decline?

### Anti-patterns

| Anti-pattern | Fix |
|--------------|-----|
| Canceling 1:1s when busy | Protect them; shorten to 20 min rather than skip |
| Only talking tickets | Park status; ask "what about the work?" |
| Surprise criticism at review | Feedback within a week of the event |
| Therapist role without boundaries | Escalate HR/wellbeing resources when needed |

---

## Feedback that lands (SBI)

**Situation → Behavior → Impact** (+ request)

> "In yesterday's design review (S), you interrupted the junior twice mid-sentence (B). They stopped contributing (I). Next time, let them finish, then add your constraint (R)."

Positive feedback uses the same structure — be equally specific.

## Performance cycle — no surprises

### At cycle start

| Artifact | Owner |
|----------|-------|
| Goals (3–5) tied to team outcomes | EM + IC |
| Level expectations (what "Senior" means here) | EM |
| Evidence folder (RFCs, incidents, mentorship) | IC |

### Mid-cycle

- Written pulse: on track / at risk / off track per goal
- Adjust goals if org priorities changed (document why)

### Review write-up (EM)

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| Outcomes | Metrics, projects shipped |
| How | Collaboration, ownership, quality |
| Growth | Skills gained; still open |
| Next level gap | Explicit: what Staff/Senior needs that is missing |
| Rating | Aligned to company rubric |

**IC self-review**: Mirror the same sections with evidence links (PRs, ADRs, postmortems).

---

## Underperformance (fair process)

1. **Clarify expectation** in writing (what good looks like)
2. **Support**: pairing, scope reduction, training, clearer tickets
3. **Time box**: e.g. 30–60 days with weekly check-ins
4. **Document**: dates, feedback, IC response
5. **Decide**: recovered / extend / exit path per company policy

Never use PIP as a surprise weapon. Never discuss performance only in Slack memes.

Deep conflict patterns: [Tech Lead Conflict and Performance](../00_Interview_Prep/Levels/Tech_Lead_Conflict_and_Performance.md).

## Promotion packets (IC)

| Evidence type | Examples |
|---------------|----------|
| Scope | Cross-team RFC adopted; on-call ownership |
| Impact | Latency −X%; incidents −Y%; cost −Z% |
| Multiplier | Mentored N engineers; raised review bar |
| Judgment | Said no with options; killed bad project |

Staff/Principal bar: [Advance Criteria](../00_Interview_Prep/Levels/Staff_Principal_Advance_Criteria.md).

## For ICs — running your side of the 1:1

Bring a doc:

```
- Wins this week (evidence)
- Asks / blockers
- Feedback for manager
- Career: one skill to practice this month
- Promotion: gap I am closing (if relevant)
```

If your manager only does status, politely reframe: "Can we use 15 minutes for career and feedback?"
