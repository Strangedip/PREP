## 1. Job switch protocol (while employed)

### Timeline (12–16 weeks typical)

| Week | Focus | Output |
|------|-------|--------|
| 1–2 | Target map | 8–12 companies; level code per firm ([Companies.md](../Core/Companies.md)) |
| 3–6 | Gap close | ROADMAP checklist for target level only; SelfAssessment ≥ band |
| 5–8 | Stories | 6 STAR stories mapped to LPs / leadership principles |
| 7–12 | Loops | 2–3 practice companies first; then dream companies |
| Offer | Negotiate | Level first, then total comp ([Comp_and_Scope](../Levels/Comp_and_Scope.md)) |

**Rule**: Do not resign until written offer + level in writing. Notice period in India (30–90 days) is a negotiation lever, not a crisis.

### Referral strategy (high signal, low spam)

| Channel | How | Don't |
|---------|-----|-------|
| **Alumni / ex-colleagues** | Specific ask: "Referral for Staff Backend, Order Platform — here's 5-line blurb + resume" | Mass LinkedIn "open to work" blasts |
| **Internal recruiters** | After referral lands; ask level calibration early | Argue level before any loop |
| **Open source / meetups** | Contribute where hiring managers watch | Fake "community" for one week |

**Referral blurb template** (paste-ready):

> Backend / platform engineer, [N] years. Owned [system] serving [X RPS / Y orders]. Led [migration / RFC] that cut [metric]. Targeting [Level] at [Company] for [team theme]. Resume + GitHub: [links].

## 2. Negotiation scripts (beyond Comp_and_Scope)

Use after verbal offer. Always negotiate **level and scope** before chasing +3L on base.

### Competing offer (factual)

> "I have a written offer from [A] at [X]L total ([base]/ / RSU / sign-on]). I'm more excited about [this team's problem], but need total compensation closer to [Z]L and confirmation of [Level code] to make the move."

### Level push (evidence-based)

> "My current scope is [cross-team platform / multi-service ownership] with outcomes [metric]. I'd like this offer evaluated at [Staff / L6] rather than [Senior / L5]. Happy to walk through two architecture stories from the loop."

### Sign-on to close RSU cliff

> "Year-1 equity is below my current unvested. Can we bridge with sign-on of [amount] over 12 months, or front-load RSU year 1?"

### Remote / hybrid (India)

> "Can hybrid / WFH days be in the offer letter? Commute and festival on-call fairness matter for retention."

## 3. Plateau diagnosis

| Symptom | Likely cause | Move |
|---------|--------------|------|
| Same title 3+ years, same ticket size | Scope not expanding | Own an RFC that crosses team boundary ([Multi-Team AR](../Levels/Multi_Team_Architecture_Review.md)) |
| Strong coder, weak interviews | No structured narrative | STAR bank + [Failure modes](../Levels/) for your level |
| Promoted in title, not scope | Title inflation | Push for Staff-shaped work or external Staff offer |
| Learning stopped | Comfort zone | Pick one stretch: HLD flagship, platform, or AI production ([05_AI](../../05_AI/README.md)) |
| "Busy but invisible" | No written artifacts | Ship ADRs / RFCs ([On-the-job RFC](../../06_On_The_Job/04_RFC_ADR_Writing.md)) |

**Self-check**: If [SelfAssessment](../Core/SelfAssessment.md) score is high but promotion is stuck, the gap is **visibility and scope**, not more LeetCode.

## 4. Burnout and recovery (engineering-specific)

| Signal | Immediate action |
|--------|------------------|
| Dreading on-call / every deploy | Ask for rotation fairness; document Sev load ([On-call](../../06_On_The_Job/03_On_Call_Incident_Response.md)) |
| No deep work for months | Block 2× 90-min focus; cut low-value meetings with EM |
| Identity = only output | Time-box job search prep; protect sleep (tracker optional) |
| Toxic manager | Document; skip-level; external options in parallel |

**Not advice to quit impulsively** — stabilize sleep and scope first, then decide with a 90-day plan.

## 5. IC ↔ EM mid-career switch

| Direction | Prep | Risk |
|-----------|------|------|
| **IC → EM** | Trial EM / TL people load; read [EM Track](../../06_On_The_Job/06_Engineering_Manager_Track.md) | Losing technical edge |
| **EM → IC** | Staff-shaped project + coding refresh; update resume with delivery + hiring as leadership | "Manager stigma" — counter with recent RFCs |

Neither path is a demotion. Comp often overlaps — compare **scope and total**, not title.

## 6. Working while prepping (sustainable load)

| Constraint | Protocol |
|------------|----------|
| Full-time job | 60–90 min weekday DSA/theory; weekend 1 mock SD |
| Notice period | Finish loops before resigning when possible |
| Festival / peak on-call | Pause applications 2 weeks; don't ghost recruiters — reschedule |

## Ready checklist (mid-career switch)

- [ ] Target level mapped via [Where am I?](../../ROADMAP.md#where-am-i-map-yourself-on-the-ladder)
- [ ] SelfAssessment meets that level's bar
- [ ] 6 STAR stories written; 2 architecture deep-dives
- [ ] Referral list of 10 humans (not 100 cold messages)
- [ ] Comp ask = level + total + non-comp (team, remote, start date)
- [ ] Resignation plan only after written offer
